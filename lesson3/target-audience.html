Target Audience

Audience:

This web site is for everyone. Everyone checks the weather before going out. We all want to know if it is going to rain or not. The weather forecast helps us plan the weeekend and our vacations.

Scenarios:


* What is the local weather?
* Is it going to rain? Do i need to bring an umbrella with me?
* What is the weather near Tenderfoot mountain? Can we go hiking?
* What is the weather forecast for the coming week? Is it going to be warm enough to start planting the crops?

Personas:

Anna Brown

Anna is a student. She and her friend are planning an outdoor activity for this weekend. She wants to know the weather. According to the weather their plans can change

James Walles

James is a farmer. He checks everyday the weather. For him it is important to know if it is going to be cold or warm. According to the weather he would work in his farm.
